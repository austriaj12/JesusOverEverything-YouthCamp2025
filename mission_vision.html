About This Camp

In a world filled with distractions that seek our attention, we are called to prioritize Jesus above all else. He offers unconditional love and invites us to live a life overflowing with hope, love, peace, and joy—all rooted in His presence.

The world may tempt us to chase after temporary wealth, fleeting happiness, and superficial satisfaction, but these pursuits pale in comparison to the eternal significance of knowing Christ.

Now is the time to consider our ways and refocus our lives on Someone who is worth living for. When we place Jesus over everything, we not only experience His transformative power in our own lives but also become powerful witnesses to others, pointing them to the true source of fulfillment and purpose.

Have you met Him? Embrace the opportunity to live for Christ, allowing His love to shine through you as a testament to the hope that transcends all worldly distractions.

Let’s entrust our lives to Jesus Over Everything!
